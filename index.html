Daily Life · Solunis
Solunis
"Every culture has a calendar. Solunis holds them all."
Solunis is a unified life-management platform built around the richness of human tradition. Whether you follow the Islamic Hijri, Hindu Panchang, Hebrew, Gregorian, or any other calendar, Solunis lets you layer them together — so your local festival and your international meeting live side by side. From scheduling appointments to sharing birthday cards and wedding invitations, it's the one app for life's most human moments.
  • 📅
    Multi-Calendar Support — Select and overlay traditional + Gregorian calendars simultaneously
  • 🔔
    Smart Reminders — Special alerts for birthdays, anniversaries, religious days, and custom events
  • 💌
    Card & Invitation Sharing — Beautiful digital cards for every occasion, shareable instantly
  • 🤝
    Appointment & Meeting Scheduler — Book and manage meetings respecting cultural contexts
  • 🌍
    Multi-Religious Awareness — Festival database covering dozens of world religions and traditions

Banking & Organisations · Placyra
Placyra
"Address is identity. Placyra makes it trustworthy."
Placyra solves one of the most fundamental problems in formal systems: knowing where a person truly lives. Built for banks, NBFCs, government departments, and any organisation needing reliable KYC, Placyra provides intelligent address verification using multiple data signals — reducing fraud, speeding onboarding, and bringing more people into the formal economy.
  • ✅
    Address Verification — Multi-layer verification using geolocation, official records, and AI
  • 🪪
    KYC Enablement — Streamline Know Your Customer workflows for any institution
  • 🛡️
    Fraud Prevention — Flag suspicious address patterns and duplicate identities
  • 🔗
    API Integration — Drop-in integration with banking and onboarding systems
  • 🗺️
    Rural & Urban Coverage — Works for both urban PIN codes and remote village addresses

Legal · LawPartner
LawPartner
"Justice should not depend on who can afford a lawyer. LawPartner levels the field."
LawPartner is an AI-powered legal guidance platform that demystifies the law for ordinary citizens. When you face a legal challenge — whether it's a consumer dispute, domestic issue, property conflict, or workplace harassment — LawPartner walks you through the relevant laws, your rights, and exactly what steps to take. It empowers you to act, not just worry.
  • 📋
    Step-by-Step Legal Guidance — Clear action plans for common legal situations
  • 📚
    Law Library — Access to relevant IPC sections, consumer laws, property laws, and more
  • 🛡️
    Rights Awareness — Understand your fundamental and legal rights in plain language
  • 📝
    Document Drafting Help — Templates for complaints, applications, and legal notices
  • 🔍
    Crime Prevention Awareness — Educate on laws that protect against specific crimes
